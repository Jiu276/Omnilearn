Chemical Balance: The Foundation of Pool Health

The secret to a healthy pool lies in maintaining proper chemical balance. Doheny's experts recommend regular testing and adjustment of key parameters to ensure safe, comfortable swimming conditions.

Essential Chemical Parameters

  • pH Levels (7.2-7.6): Maintains water comfort and prevents equipment damage. Find pH adjusters at Doheny's
  • Chlorine (1-3 ppm): Essential sanitizer that eliminates bacteria and algae. Shop chlorine products at Doheny's
  • Alkalinity (80-120 ppm): Stabilizes pH and prevents fluctuations
  • Calcium Hardness (200-400 ppm): Protects pool surfaces and equipment
  • Cyanuric Acid (30-50 ppm): Stabilizes chlorine against UV degradation

Professional-grade test kits and digital meters from Doheny's take the guesswork out of water chemistry, providing accurate readings that help you maintain perfect balance.

Troubleshooting Common Pool Problems

Even with diligent care, pools occasionally develop issues. Quick identification and treatment prevent minor problems from becoming major headaches. Doheny's comprehensive troubleshooting guides and expert support help resolve issues quickly.

Green or Cloudy Water

Usually indicates algae growth or filtration problems. Shock treatment, algaecide application, and filter cleaning typically restore clarity within 24-48 hours. Find effective pool shock treatments at Doheny's and algaecides from Doheny's.

High Chemical Consumption

Excessive chemical use often signals underlying issues like phosphates, stabilizer imbalance, or equipment problems. Regular testing identifies the root cause.

Equipment Noise or Vibration

Unusual sounds indicate potential pump or motor issues. Early intervention prevents complete failure and costly emergency replacements.
