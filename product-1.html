About Notion

Notion is a revolutionary all-in-one workspace that combines notes, tasks, wikis, and databases into a single, powerful platform. Designed for individuals and teams who want to organize their work and life in one place, Notion offers unprecedented flexibility and customization.

Why Choose Notion for Learning?

For students and lifelong learners, Notion transforms how you capture, organize, and connect knowledge. Its unique block-based system allows you to create rich, multimedia documents that can include text, images, databases, calendars, and more - all seamlessly integrated.

Database & Organization

Filtering & Sorting

Relations & Rollups

Collaboration
