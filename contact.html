Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do you choose which products to review?

We prioritize products based on user requests, market popularity, innovation, and potential impact on the learning experience. We also consider emerging technologies and tools that could benefit our community.

Do you accept paid reviews or sponsorships?

We maintain complete editorial independence. While we may receive free access to products for testing purposes, this never influences our reviews. All paid partnerships are clearly disclosed.

Can I contribute to Omnilearn as a guest writer?

Yes! We welcome contributions from educators, researchers, and experienced learners. Please reach out with your expertise and writing samples for consideration.

How often do you update your reviews?

We review our content quarterly and update reviews when there are significant changes to products, pricing, or features. Major updates are noted at the top of each review.

Do you offer consulting services?

We provide consulting services for educational institutions and companies looking to implement learning technologies. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How can I stay updated with new reviews?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or check our blog regularly. We publish new content weekly and send newsletter updates monthly.
